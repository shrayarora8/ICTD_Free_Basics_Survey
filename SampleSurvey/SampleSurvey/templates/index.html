Welcome! Lab in the Wild is a research group out of the University of Washington. We test your abilities and preferences. At the end of each experiment, you will see a page with your personalized feedback, which lets you compare yourself and your performance to other people around the world.
By participating, you contribute to research on people's similarities and differences and help improve users' experience when interacting with technology.
